Erasmus+ Experience

The Erasmus program was one of the most significant experiences of my life, both academically and personally. During my two semesters at the National Sports Academy in Bulgaria, I took a total of 23 courses, achieving AA grades in 21 of them and BB grades in the remaining 2. This academic success was a testament to my dedication and hard work. Throughout this period, I gained in-depth knowledge in areas such as sports management, sports psychology, and sports organization, and I had the opportunity to apply this knowledge in practical projects.

Erasmus was not just an academic journey but also a transformative personal experience. First and foremost, it significantly improved my English language skills. Using English daily in both academic and social settings boosted my confidence and fluency. Additionally, living in Bulgaria allowed me to learn the basics of Bulgarian, giving me the chance to explore yet another language.

Culturally, Erasmus opened my eyes to a broader perspective of the world. I met students from different countries, learned about their cultures, and shared my own. This experience enhanced my intercultural communication skills and taught me to appreciate diversity. I also had the opportunity to explore Bulgaria's rich history and natural beauty, which made my time there unforgettable.

The skills and experiences I gained during Erasmus have provided me with a significant advantage, not only in my academic life but also in my professional and personal development. The ability to interact with different cultures, improved language skills, and experience working in an international environment have laid a strong foundation for my future career goals.
