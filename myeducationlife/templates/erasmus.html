My Erasmus Experience

I had the opportunity to participate in the Erasmus+ exchange program for two semesters at the National Sports Academy in Sofia, Bulgaria. This experience not only enhanced my academic growth but also allowed me to immerse myself in a new culture and meet people from diverse backgrounds.

The Erasmus program expanded my global perspective and helped me develop essential life skills such as independence, adaptability, and intercultural communication. It also gave me the chance to practice and improve my English in a real-world environment.

I took part in various sports training modules, educational workshops, and cultural events. These experiences have enriched my personality and my professional vision as a future sports manager.
