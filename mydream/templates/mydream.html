My dream is to start earning money as a freelancer using my software skills, and then further develop myself in mobile application development and machine learning. During this process, I want to take advantage of the flexibility that freelancing offers to travel the world, meet people from different cultures, and enrich my experiences.

After completing my training in machine learning and mobile app development, I aim to establish my own mobile application and AI company and grow in this field. I envision creating innovative solutions that make people's lives easier while working with a talented team that shares my passion for technology.

This dream is not just about a lifestyle but also about creating a community where people are connected, and trust and solidarity are at the forefront. On this island, I envision a life where technology and nature coexist in harmony, and everyone can express themselves freely.
