Who is Cengizhan?

Hello,

My name is Cengizhan. Owner and creator of this web-site. I was born in Suluova, Amasya, Turkey, on November 7, 1996. I am the youngest of seven siblings in my family. My father is a retired contractor, and my mother is a homemaker. Originally, we are from Trabzon. I am a fun-loving and humorous person. In my free time, I enjoy playing the guitar, spending time with friends, going for walks, listening to music, and exercising. Sports are something I am truly passionate about—I have practiced Taekwondo professionally and played football as an amateur. Currently, I am studying Sports Management at Trabzon University.

Before this, I attended Balıkesir University, where I studied International Trade, but I did not complete the program. Later, I was accepted into Trabzon University's Sports Management program, which I am currently pursuing. I also spent two semesters at the National Sports Academy in Sofia, Bulgaria, as part of the Erasmus+ exchange program. Now, I live in Barcelona with my wife, who is from Ecuador.

I have many memories from my past and look forward to creating many more in the future. To immortalize these experiences, I designed this website. I am an active and dynamic individual, and as I've described, I strive to live life to the fullest, embracing every opportunity with enthusiasm.
