I started my professional life at a young age. At 14, I took my first step into the working world by assisting employees at a curtain shop. During my high school years, I gained short-term experience in various sectors. I worked as a car washer, a waiter at rest stops, and in agricultural jobs, which not only helped me earn money but also taught me different work disciplines.

When I began university, I worked as a waiter in cafes and as a bartender at beach clubs and bars. These roles allowed me to support myself financially while gaining in-depth experience in customer relations and the service industry. During summer breaks, I worked at 5-star tourist hotels, where I had the opportunity to serve international guests. This experience not only improved my English skills but also gave me a close look at the luxury service sector. Additionally, during my university years, I played football to earn extra income, which further strengthened my teamwork and discipline skills.

After my first university experience, I worked as a waiter at a 5-star hotel in Istanbul. Later, I transferred to Trabzon University for my second degree and started working at the DoubleTree by Hilton in Trabzon. During this time, I also worked as an assistant Taekwondo coach for two summer semesters and completed a 3-month internship at the Ministry of Youth and Sports, where I helped organize sports events.

When I went to Bulgaria as part of the Erasmus+ program, I continued working as a waiter and bartender at a hotel while pursuing my studies. This experience allowed me to work in an international environment and further improve my English. After returning, I discovered the world of software development and am currently taking courses in data analysis and web development, focusing on tools like Python (Django, SQL) and Power BI.

Throughout this journey, I have gained valuable experience in customer-focused service, sports management, and event organization. Now, I am combining my passion for sports management with my growing skills in software and data analysis, aiming to contribute to the business world in a meaningful way.
